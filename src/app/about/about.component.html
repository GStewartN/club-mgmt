About Cycling Club

Cycling Club is for all lovers of cycling. It does not matter what kind of rider you are, or what kind of bike you ride. If you love to ride, you are welcome here. From competetive racers, to mountain bikers, recumbant riders, even little kids in tricycles, we hope you will join us!

We meet every last Sunday at 7am to ride. Bring your bike, your helmet, and ride with us!
